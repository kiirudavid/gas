    About Us

    Gas Delivery Solutuon is an EPRA licensed LPG Retail delivery service. We offer a very easy to use delivery service for LPG via our call centre and mobile app “canopy gas delivery”. We are licensed by Total gas, Afrigas, K-gas, Hashi gas, Lakegas, Oilibya, Seagas, Supergas, Progas, Hunkar gas, Gas-Yetu, Jatel and Rift Gas. Our brand promise is to be your trusted source for genuine licensed products for your home and business.We also have in stock all accessories that relate to Cooking gas use for domestic purpose, this includes; Cylinder regulators, Hose pipes, Grills, Burners, Lamps etc. We are located in off Thika Superhighway Along Outering Road next to Qwetu Residence.
